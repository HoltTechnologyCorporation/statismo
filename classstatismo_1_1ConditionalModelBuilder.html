Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class statismo::ConditionalModelBuilder< T >

Creates a statistical model conditioned on some external data.

The principle of this class is to exploit supplementary information (surrogate variables) describing the samples (e.g. the age and gender of the subject) to generate a conditional statistical model as described in [2].

This class assumes a joint multivariate gaussian distribution of the sample vector and the continuous surrogates.

Categorical surrogates (e.g. gender) are taken into account by selecting the subset of samples that fit in the requested categories.

Warning
Conditioning on too many categories may lead to small or empty training sets
Using more surrogate variables than training samples may cause instabilities
See also
DataManagerWithSurrogates
